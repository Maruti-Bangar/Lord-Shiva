Visit Temple of Kedarnath

Every town and city of Uttarakhand has temples, some famous and some not so much. Haridwar, Rishikesh, Badrinath and Kedarnath are well-known temple towns but there are many more temples that tourists and devotees can visit like the Kaleshwar Mahadev and Nageshwar Mahadev temples in Lansdowne or Shiv Temple and Radha-Krishna Temple in Mussoorie

Visit the oldest temple in Uttarakhand, the Lakhamandal, dedicated to Lord Shiva, located near Dehradun or head to the biggest temple, the Tungnath, in the Rudraprayag district.
